DNA Replication üîÑ

  1. Unzipping: Helicase enzyme unwinds double helix
  2. Base Pairing: Free nucleotides attach (A-T, G-C)
  3. Joining: DNA polymerase joins nucleotides
  4. Result: Two identical DNA molecules (semi-conservative)

4. Evolution ü¶é‚ûúü¶ñ

Evolution: Change in inherited characteristics of populations over time through natural selection

Natural Selection üåø (Darwin's Theory)

  1. Variation: Organisms have different traits
  2. Competition: Struggle for limited resources
  3. Survival: Best-adapted organisms survive
  4. Reproduction: Survivors pass genes to offspring
  5. Change: Favorable traits become more common

Evidence:

  • Fossils
  • Comparative anatomy
  • DNA similarities
  • Embryology

Key Terms:

  • Adaptation: Helpful trait
  • Fitness: Survival ability
  • Selection pressure: Environmental factor

Examples:

  • Peppered moths
  • Antibiotic resistance
  • Gal√°pagos finches
