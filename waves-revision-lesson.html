What is a Wave?

A wave is a disturbance that transfers energy from one place to another without transferring matter.
üîë Key Point
Energy travels through the wave, but particles only vibrate in place
Like a stadium wave - people go up and down, but the wave moves around

How Particle Arrangement Affects Wave Speed

The speed of mechanical waves depends on how particles are arranged in the medium.
üîë Key Principle
Closer particles = faster energy transfer
Vibrations pass more quickly between tightly-packed particles
Sound speed: Solids > Liquids > Gases

Sound Speed in Different Media

üîä Sound Speed Comparison

Water (liquid): ~1,500 m/s (4√ó faster!)

Why does sound travel faster in water?

Water particles are closer together than air particles, so vibrations transfer more efficiently from particle to particle.
üí° Dolphins and whales use sound underwater because it travels far and fast!
